Lata Mangeshkar (1929-2022)

A short biography on Lata Mangeshkar

  • Lata Mangeshkar was born on 28th September 1929
  • Her real name was Hema Mangeshkar
  • Later she earned names like Nightingle of India, Voice of Millenium
  • She was a playback singer by proffesion
  • She remains single throughout her life
  • She recorded songs in over 36 indian languages
  • She gets a lot of awards in her life like National awards, Filmfare etc
  • She was honoured by Padma Bhushan, Dada saheb Phalke awards etc
  • She died on 6th feb 2022
